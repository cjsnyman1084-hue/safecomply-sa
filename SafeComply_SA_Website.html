Compliance Gaps Cost Money

Non-compliance fines under OHSA and NEMA can reach R5 million or more. An undetected gap in a legal appointment, signage register, or waste manifest exposes your business to criminal and civil liability under South African law.

📋

Generic Templates Fail Audits

Documents not referenced to the correct South African Acts, Regulations, and GN numbers are challenged and rejected on sight. DMRE, DFFE, and DoEL inspectors identify non-compliant documentation immediately.

🔍

No Warning Before an Inspection

Section 43 of the OHSA and Section 54 of the MHSA give inspectors authority to enter and shut down your operations without prior notice. Only organisations with current, properly referenced HSSEQ systems are protected.
